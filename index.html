01 / About

Biography & research interests

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence at Southwest Jiaotong University.

Before joining SWJTU, I received my Ph.D. from the Advanced Information Technology Laboratory at Kyushu University, advised by Prof. Jianjun Zhao and co-advised by Prof. Lei Ma. My work explores how analysis and testing can make emerging software systems safer and easier to trust.
